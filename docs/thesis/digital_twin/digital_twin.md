# Digital Twin Modeling for NSCLC Patients Using Longitudinal Clinical Text Embeddings for Outcome Prediction

<!-- ![Digital Twin overview](/thesis/digital_twin/digital_twin.png) -->

---

## Goal

Develop a digital twin of NSCLC patients based on embeddings derived from longitudinal clinical reports, capable of:

- Representing patient health status over time  
- Simulating disease progression under different therapies  
- Supporting downstream predictive models  

---

## Data

- Unstructured clinical text (medical reports)  
- Longitudinal tabular clinical data (e.g., treatments, clinical variables, outcomes)

---

## Project Description

This project focuses on the development of a patient-specific digital twin using vector representations (embeddings) extracted from longitudinal clinical text.
