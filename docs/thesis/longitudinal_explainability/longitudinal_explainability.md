# Explainability of Longitudinal Machine Learning Models

---

## Goal

Explore, implement and evaluate eXplainable AI (XAI) methods for longitudinal ML models (models considering evolution of clinical values during time).

---

## Data

- Type: tabular data (clinical variables + blood test results)
- Source: CART-SIE study → Italian multicenter study for patients with B-cell lymphoma treated with CAR-T immunotherapy

---

## Clinical Context

CAR-T is innovative immunotherapy with high efficacy for lymphoma, but <50% of patients achieve durable remission, and the manufacturing costs are relevant
→ XAI can identify risk factors and help improving selection of patients available for the therapy

## Project Description

- Survey XAI literature for longitudinal model explanations
- Implement selected methods
- Apply & evaluate on the CART-SIE dataset and compare approaches

---

Machine Learning fundamentals course
