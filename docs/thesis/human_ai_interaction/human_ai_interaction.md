# Human-AI interactions to built trustworthy AI agents

## Goal

Explorying, implement and evaulate human-AI interactions for agentic applications in cancer for treatment decision-making

---

## Data

Type: multimodal data both tabular and text

Source: Apollo 11 multicenter italian study for patients with lung cancer

---

## Clinical context:

Humans and AI agents are increasingly required to work together as team partners. When humans collaborate in teams, all team members take some time to get to know each other, explore how they can best work together, and eventually adapt to each other and learn to make their collaboration as fluent as possible. Their interactions play an important role in this process. It is an important research challenge to enable AI agents to participate in this dynamic process.

---

## Project Description

- survey the human-AI interaction literature
- implement interaction in agentic framework
- apply and evaluate on Apollo 11 data
